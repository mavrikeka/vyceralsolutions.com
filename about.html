Our Story

Built on decades of experience in B2B software and a passion for automation

Vyceral Solutions LLC was founded by Vikram Ekambaram and Claire Hilbert as a family-run S Corporation dedicated to transforming how B2B software companies and strategy consulting firms leverage AI to scale their operations and augment their services.

The name "Vyceral" reflects our approach: achieving visceral understanding of your business challenges, then translating that deep comprehension into tangible, measurable outcomes through intelligent automation.

What sets us apart is the unique combination of:

  • 20+ years of B2B software experience - Deep understanding of GTM challenges, consulting workflows, sales processes, and customer success
  • Expert-level AI agent platform knowledge - Mastery of AnyQuest.ai, Agent.ai, and MindStudio.ai
  • Full-stack development capabilities - Building complete web/mobile applications, not just agent workflows
  • Proven implementation track record - 70-75% time reduction across multiple engagements
  • Boutique, hands-on approach - Direct access to senior expertise on every project
