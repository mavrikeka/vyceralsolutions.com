The Challenge

Mann Partners, a boutique management consulting firm, was facing a critical bottleneck: their consultants were spending 2-3 hours per client on research before every engagement.

Key Pain Points:

  • Time Intensive: Manual research across multiple sources (LinkedIn, company websites, news, financial databases)
  • Inconsistent Quality: Different consultants produced varying levels of depth and insight
  • Capacity Constraints: Research time limited how many clients each consultant could handle
  • Data Fragmentation: Valuable insights scattered across disconnected sources
  • Scalability Issues: Growing client base meant proportionally more research hours

The Solution

Vyceral Solutions deployed a comprehensive 5-agent AI system that automated the entire research workflow while maintaining quality and depth.

The Five Specialized Agents:

1. Company Research Agent

Gathers comprehensive company information: history, structure, revenue, growth trajectory, recent news

2. Financial Analysis Agent

Analyzes financial performance, funding rounds, investor relations, market positioning

3. Competitive Intelligence Agent

Maps competitive landscape, identifies differentiators, tracks market movements

4. News & Trends Agent

Monitors recent news, press releases, industry trends, regulatory changes

5. Executive Profiling Agent

Researches key executives, their backgrounds, public statements, career trajectories

Workflow Integration:

  1. Consultant inputs client company name into Clay
  2. All five agents execute research simultaneously
  3. Findings synthesized into structured intelligence brief
  4. Results delivered in consistent format in 30 minutes
  5. Consultant reviews and adds strategic insights

Implementation Approach

A phased deployment that ensured quality and adoption

Phase 1: Discovery & Design

  • Interviewed consultants to understand research workflow
  • Mapped data sources and research patterns
  • Defined output format and quality standards
  • Selected optimal AI agent platforms

Phase 2: Agent Development

  • Built and tested each specialized agent
  • Integrated data sources and APIs
  • Developed synthesis and formatting logic
  • Created Clay orchestration workflow

Phase 3: Pilot & Refinement

  • Piloted with 3 consultants on real clients
  • Collected feedback and quality metrics
  • Refined agent prompts and logic
  • Validated time savings and accuracy

Phase 4: Full Deployment

  • Rolled out to entire consulting team
  • Provided training and documentation
  • Established ongoing monitoring and optimization
  • Measured adoption and impact metrics
