The Problem

Viaduct, an Industrial IoT platform specializing in manufacturing operations, faced a critical inflection point following their acquisition by Sumitomo Rubber Industries. The company needed to rapidly pivot their GTM strategy toward discrete manufacturing use cases and build pipeline for their CES presence—all within a tight timeframe.

Key Pain Points:

  • Strategic Pivot Under Time Pressure: Post-acquisition, Viaduct needed to demonstrate traction in discrete manufacturing quickly, with CES as a key milestone for showcasing expanded market presence
  • New Buyer Persona Education: The team needed to understand and reach Quality Directors, Manufacturing VPs, and Plant Managers—personas they hadn't systematically targeted before
  • Complex Value Proposition: Two distinct use cases needed clear articulation:
    • Field Quality: Predictive maintenance and failure prevention for deployed manufacturing assets
    • Manufacturing Excellence: MES data integration for in-process defect detection, first-pass yield improvement, and operational efficiency
  • Manual Research Constraints: Researching 500 target companies—their manufacturing challenges, quality issues, technology initiatives, and key decision-makers—would take weeks of manual effort
  • CES Attendee Targeting: The campaign needed to focus specifically on discrete manufacturing companies attending CES, requiring attendee list validation and enrichment
  • Limited Internal Resources: VP of Business Development Jim Brady would lead the initiative with selected team support, but couldn't dedicate full-time to manual research
  • Unproven Messaging: No existing discrete manufacturing messaging frameworks, case studies, or proof points validated for this new vertical

"Following our acquisition by Sumitomo, we had strong corporate support for manufacturing use cases, but we needed to prove we could generate quality pipeline quickly. CES was our target, and we had just weeks to build a repeatable outreach process for a completely new vertical."

— Jim Brady, VP Business Development, Viaduct

The Solution

Vyceral Solutions designed a focused pilot campaign using Clay, MindStudio, and Instantly to automate research and outreach for 500 discrete manufacturing decision-makers attending CES.

System Architecture & Workflow:

Phase 1: Target Account Identification & ICP Definition
  • Collaborated with Viaduct to define discrete manufacturing ICP criteria across electronics, appliances, industrial equipment, and related sectors
  • Identified target companies attending CES using attendee lists and industry intelligence
  • Loaded target companies into Clay for orchestration and enrichment
  • Used Clay's native database and Apollo integration for contact discovery
  • Identified 4-5 key decision-maker titles per company: Quality Directors, Manufacturing VPs, Plant Managers, Operations Directors, Quality Engineers
  • Built focused list of up to 500 contacts for pilot campaign validation
Phase 2: AI-Powered Company Research (MindStudio)
  • Comprehensive company research workflow:
    • Recent news, press releases, and industry developments
    • Manufacturing challenges and operational initiatives
    • Quality-related issues, recalls, or production problems
    • Technology modernization efforts and digital transformation programs
    • MES system implementations and data integration projects
  • Key stakeholder research: LinkedIn activity, company websites, conference presentations, and public statements to understand priorities and perspectives
  • Personalization point identification: For each prospect, identified 3 most relevant personalization points connecting their company's challenges to Viaduct's solutions
  • Output format: Structured research reports stored in Clay for easy sales rep access and email sequence generation
Phase 3: Personalized Messaging Development
  • Email frameworks tailored to discrete manufacturing decision-makers addressing specific pain points for each persona
  • Industry-specific value proposition messaging highlighting:
    • MES data integration capabilities for real-time visibility
    • Defect prevention and first-pass yield improvement metrics
    • Operational efficiency gains and cost reduction
    • Relevant case studies from manufacturing customers
  • Two use-case messaging tracks:
    • Field Quality Track: For Quality Directors and Field Service leaders—focused on predictive maintenance, deployed asset monitoring, and failure prevention
    • Manufacturing Excellence Track: For Plant Managers and Manufacturing VPs—focused on in-process defect detection, yield improvement, and operational efficiency
  • Personalized email sequences (4-6 touches) incorporating:
    • Company-specific research findings from MindStudio
    • Relevant manufacturing challenges identified in public sources
    • Title-specific pain points and success metrics
    • CES meeting scheduling CTAs for in-person connections
Phase 4: Campaign Setup & Integration
  • Clay workflow configuration: Automated research-to-outreach pipeline with triggers for MindStudio research and data flow to Instantly
  • Instantly integration: Connected email engagement platform for sequence deployment and tracking
  • Domain strategy coordination: Worked with Viaduct's existing Instantly domain specialist for deliverability optimization, domain health assessment, and volume planning
  • QA processes: Testing and quality checks to ensure message relevance and personalization accuracy
  • Analytics implementation: Tracking and reporting for engagement metrics, response rates, and CES meeting generation

The Integrated Workflow:

  1. Target company identification: CES-attending discrete manufacturing companies loaded into Clay
  2. Contact enrichment: Clay + Apollo discover 4-5 key decision-makers per company
  3. Company research trigger: MindStudio automatically researches each target company (manufacturing challenges, quality issues, technology initiatives)
  4. Prospect research: MindStudio analyzes key stakeholders and identifies personalization points
  5. Sequence generation: Personalized 4-6 touch email sequences created for each prospect, incorporating research findings and use-case specific messaging
  6. Campaign deployment: Sequences deployed through Instantly with optimized sending strategy
  7. Response management: Viaduct team reviews responses, qualifies meetings, and schedules CES connections

Why Clay + MindStudio + Instantly?

The ideal platform combination for time-sensitive, research-intensive manufacturing campaigns

Clay: Campaign Orchestration Hub

Why Clay was essential:

  • Central repository for target companies and enriched contact data
  • Native database + Apollo integration for discovering 4-5 decision-makers per company
  • Workflow automation to trigger MindStudio research for each account
  • Data flow management from research to email deployment
  • Seamless integration with Instantly for sequence deployment
  • ICP filtering and segmentation for focused targeting

MindStudio: Manufacturing Intelligence

Why MindStudio was the right choice:

  • Comprehensive company research: news, quality issues, manufacturing challenges, technology initiatives
  • Stakeholder intelligence from LinkedIn, company sites, and public sources
  • Personalization point identification for each prospect
  • Fast research turnaround to meet CES timeline
  • Structured output format for easy integration with Clay
  • Cost-effective for 500-contact pilot scope

Instantly: Email Engagement Platform

Why Instantly completed the stack:

  • Multi-touch email sequence deployment (4-6 touches)
  • Existing Viaduct relationship with domain specialist for deliverability
  • Domain health management and sending optimization
  • Response tracking and engagement analytics
  • Integration with Clay for automated data flow
  • Viaduct team already familiar with platform

The Focused Pilot Advantage

This engagement showcased the power of a focused pilot approach for testing new market entry. By scoping to 500 contacts, Viaduct was able to validate their discrete manufacturing GTM strategy without overwhelming their team or budget. The Clay + MindStudio + Instantly pipeline automated the heavy lifting, enabling Jim Brady to focus on strategy, messaging, and prospect engagement rather than manual research.

The results informed Viaduct's future GTM investments: they now have proven messaging frameworks, validated personas, and a repeatable process for scaling their manufacturing sector expansion beyond CES.
