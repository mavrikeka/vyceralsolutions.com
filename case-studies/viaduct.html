The Challenge

Post-acquisition market pivots demand rapid GTM execution with unfamiliar buyer personas under tight deadlines—but manual research can't deliver both speed and quality at scale.

  • Time-compressed deadlines: Fixed milestone dates (trade shows, fiscal targets) don't allow weeks of manual research
  • Unproven messaging: No validated frameworks for new vertical creates credibility risk
  • Resource constraints: Leadership time consumed by research instead of strategic work

The Learning: Focused pilots with automated research enable low-risk market validation while preserving team capacity.

The Solution

Focused pilot campaign with automated research and outreach to validate new market without overwhelming resources.

Business Outcomes:

  • 75% research time saved — Leadership freed to focus on strategy vs. manual intelligence gathering
  • Validated messaging — Real-market testing identified strongest value propositions and personas
  • Repeatable playbook — Automated pipeline reusable for future campaigns and account lists
  • Met tight deadline — Delivered on time without sacrificing quality or overwhelming team

Strategic Approach:

  • Scoped pilot (500 contacts) enables low-risk testing before full investment
  • Automated research (Clay + MindStudio + Instantly) eliminates manual bottlenecks
  • Multi-track messaging tests different personas and use cases simultaneously

The Learning: Focused pilots with automated research enable rapid market validation. Success creates a proven playbook for scaling; if results disappoint, limited investment minimizes risk.

Why Clay + MindStudio + Instantly?

The ideal platform combination for time-sensitive, research-intensive manufacturing campaigns

Clay: Campaign Orchestration Hub

Why Clay was essential:

  • Central repository for target companies and enriched contact data
  • Native database + Apollo integration for discovering 4-5 decision-makers per company
  • Workflow automation to trigger MindStudio research for each account
  • Data flow management from research to email deployment
  • Seamless integration with Instantly for sequence deployment
  • ICP filtering and segmentation for focused targeting

MindStudio: Manufacturing Intelligence

Why MindStudio was the right choice:

  • Comprehensive company research: news, quality issues, manufacturing challenges, technology initiatives
  • Stakeholder intelligence from LinkedIn, company sites, and public sources
  • Personalization point identification for each prospect
  • Fast research turnaround to meet CES timeline
  • Structured output format for easy integration with Clay
  • Cost-effective for 500-contact pilot scope

Instantly: Email Engagement Platform

Why Instantly completed the stack:

  • Multi-touch email sequence deployment (4-6 touches)
  • Existing Viaduct relationship with domain specialist for deliverability
  • Domain health management and sending optimization
  • Response tracking and engagement analytics
  • Integration with Clay for automated data flow
  • Viaduct team already familiar with platform

The Focused Pilot Advantage

This engagement showcased the power of a focused pilot approach for testing new market entry. By scoping to 500 contacts, Viaduct was able to validate their discrete manufacturing GTM strategy without overwhelming their team or budget. The Clay + MindStudio + Instantly pipeline automated the heavy lifting, enabling the leadership team to focus on strategy, messaging, and prospect engagement rather than manual research.

The results informed Viaduct's future GTM investments: they now have proven messaging frameworks, validated personas, and a repeatable process for scaling their manufacturing sector expansion beyond CES.
